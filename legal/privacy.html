Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

SMS LocalBlast is a self-hosted platform. When you deploy it on your own server, you control all data. This policy describes how the software handles data by design.

2. Data we store

  • Account data — Email address and password hash for API access.
  • Message queue — Phone numbers, message body, and status. Stored only until delivery is confirmed or failed.
  • Device metadata — Device identifiers for multi-device load balancing.

All data is stored in your Supabase database. We do not send user data to third parties.

3. Zero storage of message content (ephemeral)

Message content is held in the queue only while pending or in-flight. After status update (sent/delivered/failed), the body may be retained for logs per your database retention policy. Configure your database and backup policies as needed.

4. SSL / HTTPS

Use HTTPS in production. The software does not enforce SSL — configure your reverse proxy (e.g. nginx, Cloudflare) to terminate TLS.

5. Your responsibility

As the operator, you are responsible for:

  • Securing database credentials and API keys
  • Configuring backups and retention
  • Complying with applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

6. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact your deployment administrator or the support email configured in your instance.
